Welcome to the
29th MBA Tornament

The HEC MBA Tournament, or MBAT, is an annual sporting event
organized by HEC Paris, a leading business school in Europe, that
brings together the continent's largest annual gathering of MBA
students.

Over 1500 international future business leaders visit our campus
every May for heated competition, friendly rivalries, and
passionate celebrations. MBAT consists of sporting competitions,
festivities, and cultural exchange - all taking place on the beautiful
HEC Paris campus and select venues in the vicinity.

The MBAT is always one of the major highlights of the
calendar at HEC Paris MBA, and this year is particularly
special as we are celebrating its 29th anniversary. But far
from being just a sporting event, it’s the biggest gathering
of MBA students in Europe.

The three days of sports, events, and celebrations unite
over 1500 participants from around the world, making it an
unparalleled opportunity to network, exchange cultures,
and foster lifelong connections. The MBAT is a unique
event, an opportunity to exercise your teamwork and
leadership skills, as well as your sporting prowess. We look
forward to your arrival on campus and sharing this
fantanstic experience with you.
